ABOUT US

Our golf and leisure venue is designed to offer the perfect blend of sport, fun, and relaxation. Featuring a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range powered by Toptracer Range technology, along with a dedicated practice green and bunker, it’s the ideal place to sharpen your skills or simply enjoy the game. Visitors can unwind at our coffee shop, explore the latest equipment at the American Golf Store, or enjoy family-friendly fun on our two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur-themed crazy golf courses. As a cashless venue, we ensure convenience for all our guests. The driving range closes at 10pm, with last balls served at 9pm.
